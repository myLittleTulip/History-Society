About Us


From the Salem Witch Trials, to the history of religion, to the origins of Iran. These are the topics that fill the presentations of History Society meetings. We aspire to explore the workings of history.








Sign Up


History Society is an amazing club at Thomas S. Wootton High School. We meet up every Thursday in Room 181 to learn and discuss history.








Events


Most of our events consist of meetings, but we also plan other events as a part of our History Society. We are currently planning field trips to important historical locations to explore the events that once happened there.
