About Law of the Wild


This nonprofit organization was created to help animals. We do that by combating climate change, fighting for animal rights, and improving the conditions in which animals live in.








How You Can Help Us


You can help us by volunteering for one of our projects, or donating to one of our fundraisers. Plus, don't forget to spread the word about Law of the Wild!








Who We Are


We're a handful of volunteers with a similar goal: to end the suffering of animals at the hands of humans.
